Augmented Reality Campus Navigation System

The Idea Behind This

As freshmen, we quickly realized that navigating the vast layout of this campus can be a daunting challenge, often leading to unnecessary time and energy being wasted. To address this, we conducted extensive research and trials, culminating in the development of the NUST Augmented Reality Campus Navigation System. Designed to simplify campus navigation, this innovative system is equipped with a range of powerful features, ensuring that getting lost becomes a thing of the past.
